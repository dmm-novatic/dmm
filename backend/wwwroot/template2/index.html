Only 6.1% of Vietnam’s total population ( 100+M), currently own cryptocurrency, more than half of Vietnamese (59%) expressed interest in investing in cryptocurrencies in the future while 31% said that they would use crypto for online payments.

About DMM

How does the DMM work

DMM operates as a generated sales of products and services platform, stays in between users and dropshipping suppliers ( ex: Tiki, Lazada, Shopee, and Samsung Vietnam e-shop), online payment services providers ( ex: Payoo). When DMM drives users to spend at their app or sites, they will pay a sales commission. DMM takes a part of this money to share with buyers as cashback. Cashback can be withdrawn to a bank account or converted into ADA or C2E then be transferred to any crypto wallets on CEX and DEX exchange for earning. The amount of the C2E token will be also added to the consumer's wallet as an incentive, encouraging users to discover the crypto space.
